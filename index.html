Help Center

Got Questions?

How long does it take to build a website?
+
It typically takes 1-2 weeks for a standard business website, and 3-4 weeks for e-commerce sites, depending on complexity and requirements. Digital menus are usually ready in just 3-5 business days.
How quickly can my digital menu be ready?
+
Most digital menus are ready within 3-5 business days. You just share your menu items, prices, and dish photos — we handle the rest. QR codes are delivered print-ready for your tables.
Can I update my digital menu myself?
+
Yes! You can update prices, add new dishes, or mark items as unavailable anytime through a simple admin panel. Changes go live instantly — no reprinting, no extra cost.
Do you provide domain and hosting?
+
We can assist you in purchasing domain and hosting, but the costs are usually borne by the client unless specified in a custom package. We'll guide you through the entire setup process.
Can I update my website content later?
+
Yes! We build websites using modern frameworks or CMS that allow you to easily update content. We also offer maintenance packages for ongoing changes.
Do you offer support after delivery?
+
Absolutely. All our packages come with a specified period of free support to handle technical issues or minor changes. We're always just a WhatsApp message away.
Can you help increase my business sales?
+
Yes — that's our core focus. We don't just build websites; we build revenue-generating digital systems. From conversion-optimized landing pages to digital menus that enhance customer experience, everything we build is designed to grow your business.
Is the pricing fixed?
+
Our packages have fixed base prices, but we can customize quotes based on your specific needs and additional features. Contact us on WhatsApp to discuss your project and get a tailored quote.
